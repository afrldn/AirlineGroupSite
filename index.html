Introduction

It has never been more important for airlines to have an effective web presence. The airline industry is highly profitable. It has earned $675 Billion and is estimated to serve total 3.6 billion travelers by 2016. The top 3 companies in the airline industry, as determined by overall performance are:

  • American Airlines
  • Lufthansa
  • Delta Airlines

Though these are the top competitors between airlines, They also compete with sites like Expedia.com, Kayak.com and Travelocity.com. However, this site reviews the websites of the top three companies and review the future developents of the industry. All websites in this category share a similar purpose and features.

  1. As an etailer, selling tickets
  2. As a service, to show travel and flight information
  3. As a service for earning Frequent Flyer Miles
