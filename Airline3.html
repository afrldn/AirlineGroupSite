Delta Airlines

There are many features accompanied by the Delta Airline Website. It is very structured and easy to read and navigate. The colors are also very well coordinated with each other. Overall, the website is very classy looking, while also getting the job done in helping customers achieve what they want.

Overview & Structure

Delta has a very easily to operate website where you can sign-in conveniently, and this is located in the upper left corner. Also, it gives a text-only option for slow-loading computers, and gives disability. Fees and services, and FAQs attached above the fold. However, beneath the fold you will find first class and specialty option additions that are placed in large, high quality photos at the top of this section. And the information underneath provides you information about the sky miles card, the Delta App, and the Sky miles usages. In addition, the underneath section contains information regarding the sales to various areas and the lowest possible prices listed for each.

Positive Features

Flexibility

Allows for several ways to access information, vacation packages

Ease of Use

Side-by-Side price comparison, simplistic language select, automatic bookings search.

Simple Layout

Minimal buttons, links, and pictures make desired links easier to find.

Attractive Design

Uses high-quality photographs and complimenting colors to establish brand tone and theme.

Features to Work on

First Class Information

Information of first class benefits are located below the fold on the homepage. The customer may not find this information and still be shown first class prices.

Homepage Picture

Homepage picture changes each time the site is accessed and advertises different information, but the consumer cannot move through photos in the event that they leave and cannot find the information again.

Mobile App

Mobile application is not advertised well on site and is not automatically advertised whenever the mobile site is accessed.
