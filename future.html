Future Developments

Mobile Developent

Airlines, now more than ever, have to rely on websites, mobile apps, and social media to sustain business. Because Airlines are constantly battling for customers, web services can make or break a customers relationship with any particular company, just one bad experience, missed flight, or wrong e-mail could mean a customers leaving and bringing business to your competition

Guide Bar

The Guide Bar indicates what step customers are at in the transaction. The Guide Bar typically runs across the top of the screen after a flight is found. While we did find it overall a helpful feature when trying to purchase a ticket we also noticed that not all Airlines are using this tool the same, in fact, the Guide Bar may have been the biggest difference between the major players.
